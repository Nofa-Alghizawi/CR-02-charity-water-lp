Our Story

charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe everyone deserves access to clean water. Join us to make a difference!

Hope from a Lemonade Stand

In Ohio, 8-year-old Nora raised over $1,200 by selling lemonade to fund a water project through charity: water. That simple stand helped bring clean water to 37 people in a rural Ethiopian village. “I wanted kids like me to not have to walk hours just to drink dirty water,” Nora said.
